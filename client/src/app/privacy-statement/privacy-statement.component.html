Privacy Statement

This privacy statement explains Symarsoft portal's policies regarding what personal data is collected and processed; how it is used and/or shared with third parties and for what purposes.

Personal data this portal collects

Symartsoft collects data from you through your interaction with the portal. The collected data depends on the context of your interactions with Symartsoft and on your privacy settings. You have choices when it comes to the data you share. When portal asks you to provide your personal data you can decline. Some of the portal features require some personal data in order to function and if you choose not to provide the data you will not be able to function. In cases where the personal data is optional and if you choose not to share the data, related features like personalisation will not work for you.

How your data is used

The data we collect is used for:

  • Providing features like ability to register and login
  • Analytics, which includes sharing data with third parties that provide analytics services
  • Improvements and developement of Symartsoft features, products and services
  • Personalization of your experience
  • Advertising

Privacy Settings and Data Control

Symartsoft uses cookies and similar technologies for storing your preferences, enabling you to sign in, for analytics and advertising. You can choose not to share any of your data by:

  • Choosing not to accept cookies when the cookie banner is presented to you
  • Choosing not to use account services provided by Symartsoft
  • Changing your settings on the Manage Privacy page

In order to delete the personal data shared as part of creating an account on Symartsoft portal you can delete the the account in your account settings.
